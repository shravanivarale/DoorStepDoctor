# Anaphylaxis (Severe Allergies)

Sudden swelling of face/lips/throat, difficulty breathing, or severe hives after insect sting or food. Classify as EMERGENCY. Use EpiPen if available and trained, otherwise rush to PHC.

---
**DISCLAIMER:** This is a general triage guideline for ASHA workers. It does not replace professional medical diagnosis or treatment. Always refer critical cases to the nearest PHC immediately.
