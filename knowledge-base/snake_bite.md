# Snake Bite Protocol

Keep patient calm and immobilize the bitten limb. Do NOT cut, suck, or apply tight tourniquet. Note time of bite and description of snake if safe. Classify as EMERGENCY and transport to PHC for Anti-Snake Venom (ASV).

---
**DISCLAIMER:** This is a general triage guideline for ASHA workers. It does not replace professional medical diagnosis or treatment. Always refer critical cases to the nearest PHC immediately.
