# Respiratory Distress in Children

Check respiratory rate. Fast breathing (>50/min for infants 2-12m, >40/min for 1-5y) or chest indrawing indicates severe pneumonia. Classify as EMERGENCY. Give first dose of amoxicillin if available and transport.

---
**DISCLAIMER:** This is a general triage guideline for ASHA workers. It does not replace professional medical diagnosis or treatment. Always refer critical cases to the nearest PHC immediately.
