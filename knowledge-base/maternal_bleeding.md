# Postpartum Hemorrhage (PPH) Protocol

Severe vaginal bleeding after childbirth is life-threatening. If pad is soaked in < 1 hour, or symptoms of shock (pale, fast pulse) are present, massage uterus, escalate as CRITICAL EMERGENCY.

---
**DISCLAIMER:** This is a general triage guideline for ASHA workers. It does not replace professional medical diagnosis or treatment. Always refer critical cases to the nearest PHC immediately.
