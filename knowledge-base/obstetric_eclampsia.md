# Pre-Eclampsia and Eclampsia

Pregnant woman (>20 weeks) with severe headache, blurred vision, or seizures. Seizures indicate Eclampsia. Classify as EMERGENCY. Place in recovery position and transport.

---
**DISCLAIMER:** This is a general triage guideline for ASHA workers. It does not replace professional medical diagnosis or treatment. Always refer critical cases to the nearest PHC immediately.
