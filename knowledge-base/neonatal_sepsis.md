# Neonatal Sepsis Protocol

Infant (<28 days) with poor suckling, lethargy, fast breathing (>60/min), or chest indrawing. Fever or hypothermia. This is highly lethal. Classify as EMERGENCY and refer immediately.

---
**DISCLAIMER:** This is a general triage guideline for ASHA workers. It does not replace professional medical diagnosis or treatment. Always refer critical cases to the nearest PHC immediately.
