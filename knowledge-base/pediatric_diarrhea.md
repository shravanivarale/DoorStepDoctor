# Pediatric Diarrhea & Dehydration

Assess signs of severe dehydration: lethargy, sunken eyes, inability to drink, extremely slow skin pinch. If severe, class as EMERGENCY for IV fluids. Otherwise, start ORS and zinc.

---
**DISCLAIMER:** This is a general triage guideline for ASHA workers. It does not replace professional medical diagnosis or treatment. Always refer critical cases to the nearest PHC immediately.
